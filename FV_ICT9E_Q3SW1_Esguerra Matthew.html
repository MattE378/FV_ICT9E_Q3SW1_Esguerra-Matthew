The Flash

Introduction

After being struck by lightning, Barry Allen wakes up from his coma to discover he's been given the power of super speed, becoming the Flash, and fighting crime in Central City.
Barry Allan/The Flash
The Flash, or Scarlett Speedster, has the ability of superspeed, which allows him to run faster than light speed.
Zoom
Hunter Zolomon was better known as Zoom, a speedster super-villain and enemy to the Flashes.
Reverse Flah
Reverse-Flash is unwavering in his determination to oppose and destroy all that's important to the Flash.
